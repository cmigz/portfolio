About Me

Hey, welcome to my portfolio. My name is Christian.

Coder | Entrepreneur | Gamer


When I discovered my passion for coding I had realized that I had finally found what I was meant to do in this life. I employ a personal philosophy of always working to improve one's self in addition to treating others as I'd like to be treated. I come from a hard working old school Italian family where going above beyond is the standard, and look forward to applying that work ethic to the world of web development.


I want to give you an idea of who I am, I think this short story will suffice. I was diagnosed with Bacterial Meningitis when I was two, the result was a childhood in and out of the hospital that culminated in the choice to amputate my left foot when I was thirteen. When it comes down to it I consider this to be one of the greatest blessings in my life. Had I not struggled through that I wouldnt be the determined, focused, and rounded person I am today.


On a more enjoyable note I am a huge video game and comic book afficianado. I've been playing since I was a kid, and its always been a passion. While I was never going to be the start quarterback I could kick anyone's ass in super smash brothers. Comics has only recently become one of my greater loves in this life, but when I dove in I dove deep. Going on three years now my cousin and I sell comics at the cons that come to town and those weekends are often my favorites times of the year.
